About

Wellbean Tofu, located in Duluth, Georgia, is Atlanta's go to for Korean soon dubu (tofu stew). Through our service and our great selection of side dishes, we devote ourselves to offer our guests an unforgettable experience.

Our tofu stew comes in many flavors. The magic is in the soft tofu and bubbling hot stew. Enjoy cracking your egg in your stew and eating stone pot cooked rice with a cup of traditional rice barley tea.
